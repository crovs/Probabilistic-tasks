Why 220?

  • There are 12 labeled balls in the urn.
  • Order ignored means we choose a 3-ball set.
  • That is the combination count C(12,3).

Why 70 And 6?

  • 70 counts labeled 3-ball samples with exactly two red.
  • 6 counts only color sequences with exactly two red.
  • Different recording rules create different kinds of outcomes.

What Changes?

  • Task 1-2 keep exact ball identities and ignore order.
  • Task 3-4 ignore identities and keep color order.
  • The draw is the same, but the mathematical outcome changes.
