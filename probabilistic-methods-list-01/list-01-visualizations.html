What One Outcome Is

(x, theta)
  • x: distance from the center to the nearest line
  • theta: angle between the needle and the lines
  • This is continuous, not a finite list of cases

What The Animation Shows

  • Red needle: intersects a line
  • Green needle: misses the lines
  • As throws increase, empirical probability gets closer to theory

What To Say In Class

  • "One outcome is the pair (x, theta)."
  • "The event is x <= (L/2) sin(theta)."
  • "The simulation illustrates the exact formula 2L / (pi d)."
